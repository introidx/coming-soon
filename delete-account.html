Account & Data Deletion Request

Oktagn  ·  Last updated: 18 July 2026

At Oktagn, we value your privacy and give you full control over your data. Because our app uses Google or Apple sign-in, we only store the essential data required to manage your account and track your training progress.

This page explains how to permanently delete your Oktagn account, what we remove, and how long it takes.

How to request deletion

Option 1 — Email

Send us a deletion request

Email us from the same account you signed in with (your Google or Apple address), using the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

Email admin@octagn.in

Sending from your registered address lets us verify the request is genuinely yours. If you email from a different address, we may ask you to confirm your identity before we proceed.

How long it takes

Requests are processed manually within 7 business days of receipt. We will email you to confirm once your account and data have been deleted.

What data is deleted

Upon receiving your request, we permanently delete:

  • Your unique user ID and the identifier linking your account to your Google or Apple sign-in.
  • Your profile information — name, email address, and profile picture URL.
  • Your fitness profile — height, weight, goals, and onboarding assessment results.
  • All in-app activity — workouts, challenges, exercises, sets, reps, completion history, and streaks.
  • Your app preferences and personalisation settings.

What data is retained

  • We do not retain any personal data after deletion is complete.
  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Your training history and streaks cannot be recovered afterwards, so please export or note anything you want to keep before requesting deletion.
  • If you later want to use Oktagn again, you are welcome to sign up for a new account — it will start fresh with no previous data.

Questions

If you have any questions about deleting your account or how we handle your data, contact us at admin@octagn.in, or read our Privacy Policy.
